Lasagna

Wow, you haven't tried anything like this ultimate lasagna!

You're sat at home. You're alone. And suddenly you feel like it's lasagna time!

If this has ever happened to you, believe me this is the recipe for you and you'll find all the steps needed to create the ultimate lasagna below.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Cheese
  • Egg
  • Noodles

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
