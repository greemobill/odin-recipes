Stir Fry

Throw everything in your fridge into this healthy, zesty stir fry mix!

This is the one... the best stir fry you may ever make

Throw away all your other recipe books, all you need is rice, your choice of protein and some old veg from the back of the fridge. Throw in anything else you like!

Ingredients

  • vegetable oil
  • beef
  • broccoli
  • 3 x red peppers
  • onion
  • soy sauce
  • garlic
  • rice

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients and chop into fine bits
  2. Heat vegetable oil in wok
  3. Add everything to the pan
  4. Begin boiling the rice in an adjacent pot
  5. When everything is nice and crispy, dollop in the soy sauce and serve on the bed of rice
