Jacket Potato

A healthy pack of potatoes goes a long way with this hearty recipe. Boil em, mash em or jacket em!

It's lunch time. Your stomach is growling. Your only tool is a microwave.

It's tattie time!

Ingredients

  • 6 medium well-rounded tatoes
  • vegetable oil
  • salt
  • butter
  • cheese
  • herbs of your choice

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients and set microwave
  2. Drizzle oil over potatoes
  3. Microwave tato for roughly 7 minutes
  4. Grate cheese and chop herbs in meantime
  5. When potato is done in the microwave, spread butter and sprinkle over your cheese and herbs
  6. Nosh!
